Key Strategic Findings — Applied to Client Content Plans

01
Value-first content consistently outperforms promotional posts — recommended a content mix of 70% educational or storytelling content, 30% promotional to build trust before selling.
02

Responding to comments within the first hour significantly boosts organic reach — built an engagement protocol into client content delivery to ensure post-publishing follow-through.

What Is a Lazy Bum? A True Definition.

"What is a Lazy Bum?" he asked, trembling from the pressure of my nobility, my profound laziness, and the biting cold. It was a freezing night in mid-December. Everything had turned a murky shade of white. Cars were smothered in snow and the streets were eerily quiet. Even the commoners were too afraid to venture out.

I, however, had not moved from my position on the sofa in approximately eleven hours. This, I told him, is the answer to your question.
